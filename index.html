About Butwal Multiple Campus

Butwal Multiple Campus (BMC), established in **2030 B.S. (1973 A.D.)**, is a leading public educational institution in Nepal. Affiliated with Tribhuvan University, BMC offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines. The campus is well-known for its academic excellence, experienced faculty, and affordable tuition fees.

Located in **Butwal, Rupandehi**, the campus provides modern infrastructure, including a **well-equipped library, advanced computer labs, and research facilities**. The institution fosters a dynamic learning environment, ensuring students receive both theoretical and practical knowledge to excel in their respective fields.

BMC is committed to **providing scholarships** to deserving students and conducts various **extracurricular activities, seminars, and workshops** to enhance students' professional and personal growth.

Courses Offered

Explore a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties:

  • Faculty of Humanities & Social Sciences: BA, MA (English, Nepali, Sociology, Economics, Political Science)
  • Faculty of Science: B.Sc. (General Science), B.Sc. CSIT (Computer Science & Information Technology)
  • Faculty of Management: BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration), BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies), MBS (Master of Business Studies)
  • Faculty of Education: B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education), M.Ed. (Master of Education)
  • Faculty of Law: LL.B (Bachelor of Laws - 3 years), BA LL.B (Integrated Law Program - Upcoming)
